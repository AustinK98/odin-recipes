Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This is a childhood comfort food of mine. It has all a growin' boy, or gal' needs. Packed with savory ground beef, cream of mushroom, topped with crisp tater tots to bring it all together.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of ground beef, more fat equals a juicier casserole, while lean will be a healthier route
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese, this can be supplemented with any cheese but cheddar works great
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup, if you enjoy a saltier taste you can supplement creamed beef
  • 1 tbspn cup of ketchup
  • 1 tbsp of worcestershire sauce
  • 3 cups of frozen tatertots, chances are you're gonna have leftovers, you can get creative for sides!
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F or 218 C
  2. In a large pan, brown the beef and onions over medium-high heat, break down the meat as it cooks and drain off excess juices from pan
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the beef/onions with cream of mushroom,ketchup, worcestershire sauce, and 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Lightly oil/grease your shallow baking pan and pour the mixture into the dish
  5. Top with an even layer of tater tots
  6. Bake at 425F or 218 c for 20 minutes
  7. After 20 minutes, top with your other cup of shredded cheese, and bake an additional 5 minutes to melt your cheese
  8. Let cool and enjoy!
